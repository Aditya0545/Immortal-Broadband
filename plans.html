Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about our plans

What is the installation process?

Our technician will visit your location, install the necessary equipment, and ensure everything is working perfectly. Installation typically takes 1-2 hours.

Can I upgrade or downgrade my plan?

Yes, you can change your plan at any time. Upgrades take effect immediately, while downgrades take effect from the next billing cycle.

What if I'm not satisfied with the service?

We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee. If you're not satisfied within the first 30 days, we'll refund your payment in full.

Do you offer any discounts for long-term commitments?

Yes, we offer discounts for annual payments. Contact our sales team to learn about available discounts for your chosen plan.
