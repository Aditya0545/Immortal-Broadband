About Immortal Broadband

Connecting communities with high-speed internet and innovative services

Who We Are

Immortal Broadband is dedicated to providing comprehensive solutions for all current and future internet and private network needs. Our clientele spans individual consumers, small and medium-sized enterprises, and startups.

The term 'broadband' denotes high-speed and high-bandwidth communication infrastructure, particularly focusing on rapid internet services, a crucial aspect for both consumers and governments.

In this rapidly evolving digital landscape, high-speed internet has become an essential utility. Governments and consumers alike prioritize accessibility to high-speed internet. The advent of groundbreaking satellite-based broadband services has the potential to revolutionize global access, promising a brighter, more connected future.

Leveraging our extensive experience and expertise across diverse markets, we manage a technologically advanced proprietary network spanning approximately 1,000 km of fiber. This network encompasses two major regional systems in Bihar and Jharkhand, positioning us at the forefront of broadband connectivity.

Our Mission

To empower individuals and businesses with seamless, high-speed internet connectivity, fostering digital inclusion and driving innovation across Bihar and Jharkhand.

Our Vision

To be the leading internet service provider, recognized for reliability, customer-centricity, and pioneering advanced broadband solutions that shape a connected future.

Our Journey

Milestones in our mission to connect communities

2010

Inception

Immortal Broadband was founded with a vision to bridge the digital divide in regional India, starting with core services in Bihar.

2015

Network Expansion

Expanded our fiber optic network to cover key cities in Jharkhand, significantly increasing our reach and service capacity.

2018

Introducing OTT Packs

Launched integrated entertainment packages, bundling high-speed internet with popular OTT platforms to enhance customer value.

2023

Future Ready

Upgraded infrastructure to support speeds up to 500Mbps and beyond, preparing for the next generation of digital demands.
